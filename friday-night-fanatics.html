Fair Starting Positions

Rounds start from connected, fair positions so kids work right away without chasing, stalling, or rough mismatches. The goal is skill development and growth, not winning a trophy.

Starting examples may include:
  • Shoulder to shoulder on the knees
  • Seated guard connection
  • Double octopus guard
  • Other coach-selected positions
