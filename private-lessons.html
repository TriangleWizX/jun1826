Why Kingston Private Lessons Are Structured Differently

Tannersville sessions happen at the Sensei Sandy BJJ studio, which keeps them simple, focused, and the best-value option.

Kingston Add-On Privates are scheduled near existing Kingston classes. Since Sandy is already teaching in Kingston, the private lesson can work as a convenient add-on session.

Kingston Standalone Travel Privates happen outside normal Kingston teaching blocks. These require a separate trip, a larger reserved schedule window, gas, vehicle wear, and facility coordination.
