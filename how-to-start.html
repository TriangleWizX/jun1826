How to
Start

Simple path: 6 weeks to try it, 12 weeks to turn it into a habit.
No experience needed. No “must get in shape first.”

View Programs
Step 1

Pick your 6-week program

Choose the best fit for your family. This is the entry ramp for everyone.

Kids (Ages 4–11)

6-Week Confidence Challenge

Focus, discipline, and anti-bullying skills in a safe environment.

Teens (Ages 11–15)

6-Week Resilience Challenge

Real-world self-defense, athletic conditioning, and peer support.

Adults (16+)

6-Week Strong & Safe Kickstart

Technical instruction, functional fitness, and stress relief.

Let's get you confirmed

Claim Your Free Class - OR - Call or Text Us

We’ll confirm your start date, answer questions, and plug you into the class time that works best for your week.

Step 2

Save your spot

Two easy ways to start.

You don't need to buy gear or pay a registration fee yet. Just let us know you are coming so we can prepare for your arrival.

  • Send a quick text or call using the number on the site.
  • Fill out the short interest form (via the button).
Step 3

Arrive 10 minutes early

No one is left guessing on the edge of the mat.

Timing

Show up 10 minutes early for a quick tour and safety check-in.

Wear

Comfy gym clothes (t-shirt and shorts/leggings). No zippers preferably.

Bring

A water bottle and a positive attitude. We handle the rest.

Step 4

Decide on your next 12 weeks

Near the end of your 6-week program, we’ll do a short check-in.

Loving it?

Choose a 12-week plan that fits your goals and budget.

Not the right fit?

You walk away happy, with better skills and zero hard feelings.
