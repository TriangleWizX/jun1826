What to bring

Why Go Private?

Some things are easier to fix when it’s you, a clear plan, and a coach watching every detail.

Is this a good fit for you?

  • You’re new to Jiu-Jitsu and want a confidence boost before joining group classes.
  • Your child does better 1-on-1 or needs a quieter setting to stay focused.
  • You feel stuck on the same problems again and again.
  • Your work schedule is hectic and you need training times that match real life.

What We Work On

We identify the "stuck spots" in your game and clear the path for progress.

  • Escape Drills from Bad Positions

    Learn to breathe, frame, and recover guard when you are under heavy pressure.

  • Sharper Pressure Passing

    Learn how to use your weight distribution to melt through guards without getting swept.

  • Focused Problem Solving

    Build a simple plan you can use during sparring.
