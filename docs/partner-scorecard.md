## Metrics Per Partner

- `book_intro_click`
- `book_intro_submit`

## Attribution Rules
- Source of truth: UTM on booking URL (`utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`)
- If UTM missing, classify as `website / organic / evergreen`

## Monthly Actions
- Keep: partners with positive submit trend and steady show-rate proxy
- Improve: partners with high clicks and low submit rate (message mismatch)
- Pause: partners with low sessions and no submits for 2+ cycles
