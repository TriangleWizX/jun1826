Recommended Page-Length Rule
Acquisition pages

Homepage, Kids, Teens, Adults:

One promise, one audience, one offer, one primary action.

Decision pages

Schedule, Pricing, Bio, Success Stories:

Answer the specific comparison question and return the visitor to the Free Intro.

Utility pages

Directions, Contact, Show-Up Kit, Waiver:

Complete the task quickly. Remove educational exits and repeated sales sections.

Resource pages

Glossary, videos, blog, stretches:

Educate first, then use one relevant CTA near the end.
