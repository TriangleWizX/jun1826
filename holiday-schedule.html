Holiday Schedule FAQ

Are regular classes held on major holidays?

Usually, the academy closes on major family holidays such as Memorial Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year's Day.

Are there kids classes during school breaks?

Sometimes. When families want a structured activity during a break, we may offer short kids sessions or holiday camps.

Do I need to sign up ahead of time?

Yes. Holiday classes and camps require advance sign-up so we can plan staffing, class size, and safety.

What happens if a paid holiday class is canceled?

If Sensei Sandy BJJ cancels a paid holiday special, families receive a credit or refund.

Where should I check first?

Check this page first, then confirm by text if you are planning around a holiday week.
