Safety

Beginner Lane keeps jiu-jitsu pacing clear, partners matched, and instruction calm so you can practice control from day one.

What to wear

Wear athletic gear you can move in. Shorts, t-shirt, or a jiu-jitsu gi if you already have one.

Time & frequency

Start with two sessions per week when possible. Many families train Mon/Wed or Tue/Fri for steady jiu-jitsu momentum.
