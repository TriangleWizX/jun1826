Whizzer

A whizzer is a strong overhook used to counter underhooks, disrupt posture, and make takedowns or body-lock entries harder to finish.

Intermediate conceptBasicsOverhook Underhook Body Lock

Quick definition

A whizzer is a strong overhook used to counter underhooks, disrupt posture, and make takedowns or body-lock entries harder to finish.

Beginner translation

A whizzer is an overhook that acts like a defensive steering wheel. It helps turn your partner out of position.

Why it matters

A whizzer is a strong overhook used to counter underhooks, disrupt posture, and make takedowns or body-lock entries harder to finish.

What beginners should know

A common mistake with whizzer is using it passively instead of with structure and timing.

FAQ

Is a whizzer any overhook?

No. A whizzer is an overhook used with stronger defensive pressure and angle.

Does the arm alone make the whizzer work?

No. Hips, feet, posture, and angle make the whizzer effective.
