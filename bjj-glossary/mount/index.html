What mount means in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Mount is a strong top position where one person sits over the other person’s torso.

Foundational concept Positions Common Start here Bridge Frame Side Control

Quick definition

Mount is a strong top position where one person sits over the other person’s torso.

Beginner translation

The person on top has control. The person on bottom needs to stay calm, protect themselves, and escape step by step.

Why it matters

Mount is one of the most important control positions in Jiu Jitsu. It teaches pressure, balance, defense, and escape timing.

What beginners should know

Being mounted can feel uncomfortable at first. The answer is not panic. The answer is frames, breathing, bridging, and careful escape work.

Common class phrases

  • Hold mount.
  • Escape mount.
  • Bridge and turn.
  • Keep your elbows in.
  • Stay heavy on top.

Beginner Safety Cue

Safety cue: Move with control and communicate early while training mount.
