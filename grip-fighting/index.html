Grip Fighting

Grip fighting is the battle to get the connections you want while stopping your partner from getting the connections they want.

Foundational concept Movements Common Grip Guard Transition

Quick definition

Grip fighting is the battle to get the connections you want while stopping your partner from getting the connections they want.

Beginner translation

It is the small hand battle that often decides what happens next.

Why it matters

BJJ often gets easier once beginners realize the battle starts before the sweep or submission.

What beginners should know

A common mistake with grip fighting is using it passively instead of with structure and timing.

Common class phrases

  • You see it standing
  • inside guard
  • during passing
  • and in almost every gi exchange before a bigger move happens

Beginner Safety Cue

Safety cue: Move with control and communicate early while training grip fighting.
