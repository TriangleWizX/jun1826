Class Schedule

Monday

6 PM - 8 PM

Monday Night Curriculum: Our White Belt Classes for the beginner to novice Jiu-Jitsu players. We provide basic movements incorporating exercises that improve strength, stamina, and flexibility. You'll develop the foundations of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Self Defense. Also available Thursday evenings at 7pm.

Tuesday

7:30 AM - 9 AM, 5 PM - 8 PM

Tuesday Class Curriculum: Advanced classes on Tuesday caters to ranks Blue through Black Belts. Also Teens at 5pm. Expect a dynamic session, focused on sparring, starting from standing positions. Ideal for those preparing for competitions. This class sharpens your Jiu-Jitsu tactics and strategy. Anyone aiming to enhance their skills is welcome. Expect intense, fulfilling training.

6 PM - 8 PM

Thursday Curriculum Our mixed levels classes welcomes everyone. We focus on two Jiu-Jitsu techniques in the Gi, then spar for 30 minutes. Perfect for learning and practicing, BPMs provided. We follow with White Belt class and additional resources for students seeking extracurricular training.

Sabado Gigante: Designed for beginners ready to grow. Here, we refine your foundational skills and boost your practical understanding of BJJ to start the week.
