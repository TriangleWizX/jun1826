Family-Focused Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Arrives in Tannersville on August 1

Sensei Sandy BJJ empowers kids, teens, and adults to build confidence - together

TANNERSVILLE, N.Y. | July 25, 2025. Mountain Top families will soon have a new way to get fit, have fun, and grow closer: Sensei Sandy BJJ opens its doors on Friday, August 1, offering Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes just steps from Main Street.

“Our goal is simple: confidence through connection,” said Sandy Jose Núñez, local resident, black-belt instructor, and founder of the program. “Whether you’re seven or seventy, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu teaches discipline, empathy, and resilience - the life skills that keep small communities strong.”

Located at 6045 Main Street, Tannersville, the academy will run three age-based sessions every Friday:

ClassTime
Kids (ages 5-11)4:00 p.m.
Teens (12-16)5:00 p.m.
Adults (18+)6:00 p.m.

Opening-day open house

  • Watch or join a beginner class
  • Healthy snacks and door-prize raffle (including a free uniform)
  • Sign-up bonus: two bonus classes during launch week

Why It Matters

  • First BJJ program on the Mountain Top: Gives rural families access to a fast-growing martial art without long drives down the mountain.
  • All-levels welcome: No prior experience required; classes emphasize safety, play-based learning, and mutual respect.
  • Community-first mission: Students mentor one another, strengthening local ties beyond the mats.

About Sensei Sandy BJJ

Sensei Sandy BJJ is a family-oriented Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program committed to empowering Catskills residents of all ages through safe, inclusive martial-arts training. Head instructor Sandy Jose Núñez brings 15 years of NYC teaching experience and a passion for community building to Tannersville.
