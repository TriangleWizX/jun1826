BJJ Anatomy: Skeleton Basics

Kids don’t just roll around – they learn how their bodies work. We use this poster in class so students can point to their hips, ribs, spine, and shoulders and talk about what they feel.

  • Teaches real names for major bones and joints.
  • Makes safety talks easier: “my elbow feels tight,” not “my arm hurts.”
  • Kid-friendly, game-style artwork they recognize and remember.
