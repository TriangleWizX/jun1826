30-Day Confidence Guarantee

We want every student—kids, teens, and adults—to begin training with complete confidence. That is why we offer a straightforward, risk-free 30-Day Confidence Guarantee.

Here is how the guarantee works:

  • Attend Consistently: Train actively and attend classes consistently during your first 30 days of the semester program.
  • Decide if it Fits: If, after participating consistently, you decide our program is not the right fit for you or your child, contact Sandy directly within your first 30 days.
  • Get it Solved: We will work with you to make it right, or we will refund your program payment.

Our goal is to build a room of steady progress, mutual respect, and safe training. This guarantee is designed to let you experience our training culture first-hand without worry.
