Video Library

Sensei Sandy BJJ videos rooted in Tannersville, NY

Sensei Sandy BJJ is a calm Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu studio on Tannersville's Main Street, and this page shares what our mats look like when the Catskill sun hits the windows. We record weekly clips from kids, teens, and adults so you can see how we keep the lane friendly, no-gi or gi, while staying courteous, safety-first, and rooted in the mountains.

This page is the beginner-friendly library that anchors every Catskills clip with naming, location context, and the coaching tone you will feel when you step into the studio. Watch the mic'ed cues, notice how partners check in, and follow the quiet energy so you can immediately recognize "Sensei Sandy BJJ" when you visit.

Use these videos before your Free Intro to know what to expect, share them with nervous kids so they can imagine the calm mat, and choose the lane that matches your travel plans. Pause to study warm-ups, see how we handle consent, and track how families from the village, ski lodge, or art walk juggle training with mountain life.

Between gallery nights, ski shifts, and mountain trails, every clip reminds you that this studio is in Tannersville. The videos stay honest so you know this mat, these coaches, and the community before you even drive up Route 23A.
