6. Teen Beginner FAQ

Do I need any experience?

No. Most teens start with zero experience. Sandy introduces the basic safety rules, partner care, and essential movements at a beginner-safe pace during their first class.

Is it safe?

Yes. Safety is our top priority. Sandy coaches grappling-only training with clear boundaries, partner matching, and progressive resistance as skills develop.

What should I bring?

Just comfortable athletic clothes (like shorts and a t-shirt). We provide a clean loaner uniform (Gi) for the Free Intro. No special gear is required.

What if my teen needs a calm start or misses a class?

Consistent days build the best progress, but families can easily reschedule. Just send Sandy a text to let him know, and we'll find a makeup time that works. Sandy will walk them through the room and let them go at their own pace.

Can parents watch?

Yes. Parents are always welcome to stay, sit on the sideline, watch the entire class, and talk with Sandy after training.

How do I get started?

The best first step is to book a Free Intro. We'll show you around the room, walk through the safety basics, and help your teen choose the next step.
