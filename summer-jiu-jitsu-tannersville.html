Weekly Progression

A calm summer confidence progression.

The first 4 weeks build the core confidence cohort routine. Weeks 5 and 6 help your child keep building momentum.

Week 1 · Jun 30 / Jul 2

Safe Start

Your child learns the room, the rules, the coach, and the class rhythm.

Goal: Walk in, join the mat, and feel oriented.

Week 2 · Jul 7 / Jul 9

Body Confidence

Your child practices balance, base, falling safely, getting up, and simple movement.

Goal: Move with less fear and more control.

Week 3 · Jul 14 / Jul 16

Partner Confidence

Your child works with carefully matched partners through short, coached activities.

Goal: Practice cooperation, boundaries, and calm effort.

Week 4 · Jul 21 / Jul 23

Confidence Check

We review progress, celebrate small wins, and give parents a simple update.

Goal: Show visible growth and choose the next best step.

Week 5 · Jul 28 / Jul 30

Smart Choices

Your child practices tap culture, partner care, listening, and responsibility.

Goal: Make safe choices with less coach prompting.

Week 6 · Aug 4 / Aug 6

Put It Together

Your child finishes with a clearer sense of progress, routine, and confidence.

Goal: Leave with a next-step plan.
