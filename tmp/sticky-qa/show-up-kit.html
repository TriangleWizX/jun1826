2-Minute Checklist

Before You Leave

1

Arrive 10 minutes early

We’ll give you a quick tour, check your goals, and help you settle in.

2

Wear athletic clothes

Shorts and a t-shirt are perfect. Choose clothes without zippers.

3

Bring water

Simple is best. Water, clean clothes, and a calm first visit.

4

Use Main Street parking

Park nearby, enter by the Hunter Foundation Antique Shops, and head upstairs.

5

Finish the waiver
