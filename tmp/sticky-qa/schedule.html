First Visit Info

Show up made easy.

Everything about starting, safety, and after-intro details.

Safety

Beginner Lane keeps pacing clear, partners matched, and instruction calm so you can practice control from day one.

What to wear

Wear athletic gear you can move in. Shorts, t-shirt, or a gi if you already have one.

Time & frequency

Start with two sessions per week when possible. Many families train Mon/Wed or Tue/Fri for steady momentum.
