Questions

Adult Beginner FAQ

Can I start from my current fitness level?

Yes. Your first visit is paced so you can learn the room, safety rules, and basic movement comfortably.

What kind of training happens in the first class?

Your first class is skill-first. You’ll learn safety, tapping, beginner-paced drills, and how the room flows before adding more intensity over time.

What should I wear?

Wear athletic clothes you can move in. Bring a gi if you have one. Starting fresh? Come simple and we’ll guide you.

What happens after the Free Intro?

Most adults choose the 12-week Core Culture track after their first visit because it gives them structure, consistency, and a clear training rhythm.

Can I reschedule?

Yes. Text Sandy if plans change and we’ll help you find your next best class.
