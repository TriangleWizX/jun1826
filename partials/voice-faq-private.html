Questions people ask before booking private lessons

What happens in a private lesson?

We run a movement check, focus one core position, and finish with a simple next-step plan.

Can my child start with private lessons before group class?

Yes. Many families use one or two private sessions as a calm on-ramp.

Do private lessons remove all injury risk?

No training is risk-free, but private coaching lets us control pace, partner load, and progression.

Are you the same school as the Modesto Sensei Studio?

No. Sensei Sandy BJJ is in Tannersville, New York and is not affiliated with the Modesto school.
