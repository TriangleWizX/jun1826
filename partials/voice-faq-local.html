Questions people ask before booking

Where do I park?

Street parking is easy on Main Street in Tannersville and nearby side streets. Arrive 5 to 10 minutes early for a calm start.

What should I wear?

For your Free Intro, wear comfortable athletic clothes with no zippers or buttons. We cover gear options after class.

How do I start?

Start with a Free Intro: quick tour, safety walkthrough, and your first class at a beginner-safe pace. If you need to reschedule, just text.
