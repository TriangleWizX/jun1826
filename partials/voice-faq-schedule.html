Questions people ask before booking

What are the class times in Tannersville?

Private lessons are available at 4:00 PM, youth class starts at 5:00 PM, and adult class starts at 6:00 PM at 6045 Main St, 2nd Floor, Tannersville.

Which days are Gi and No-Gi?

Gi classes run Monday, Tuesday, and Friday. No-Gi runs Wednesday plus Saturday No-Gi blocks.

Can I reschedule if plans change?

Yes. You can cancel or reschedule by text.

Can I start as a complete beginner?

Yes. Beginner Lane starts calm with coached rounds and clear pacing.
