Questions people ask before booking

Can I start as a complete beginner?

Yes. Beginner Lane starts calm with coached rounds and clear pacing.

Is this striking or grappling?

This is grappling-only Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. No punches, kicks, or striking rounds.

Can parents watch class?

Yes. Parents are welcome to watch and ask questions after class.

Can I reschedule if plans change?

Yes. You can cancel or reschedule by text.
