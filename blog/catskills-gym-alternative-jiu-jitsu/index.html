Safety-First FAQ for Catskills Gym Seekers

I'm looking for a gym with weights - is this for me?

Yes. We use bodyweight mastery, leverage, and resistance to build functional strength that carries into daily life. You do more than lift; you learn to move with purpose.

I have zero coordination or I am old.

You are exactly who the Intro Curriculum is designed for. We start slow, scale every drill, and prioritize longevity so you can grow safely at any age. Browse the beginner BJJ glossary.

Is it clean?

Absolutely. Mats are sanitized after every class, common-touch surfaces are disinfected daily, and we enforce clean-gear and footwear rules to keep the space fresh and safe.
