In smaller mountain towns, most dedicated Brazilian Jiu Jitsu programs are centralized. For Windham families, that location is Tannersville.

Students from Windham, Hunter, and Haines Falls train together in one structured program rather than fragmented small groups.

This creates:

  • Clear progression for beginners

  • Reliable class times

Beginner Friendly Matters More Than Location

Most people don’t quit because of distance. They quit because the first experience is overwhelming.

A good first class should include:

  • A quick tour of the space
  • A safety walkthrough
  • A clear first lesson
  • Train at your own pace
