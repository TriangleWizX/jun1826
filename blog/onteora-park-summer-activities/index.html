Beyond the Gates: The Counter-Balance to Leisure

Onteora Park preserves tradition - a sanctuary for the arts, tennis, and the quiet stewardship of the Catskills. Yet the modern resident knows that relaxation without challenge leads to atrophy.

While the Club perfects your social capital, Tannersville's Main Street houses a hidden forge designed to sharpen your human capital. For those seeking a cognitive equal to their professional complexities, the answer lies five minutes from the gate.

For the Legacy (Kids Lane)

Summer offers freedom, but freedom without structure breeds complacency. Our curriculum provides the necessary counter-weight to Onteora's ease. For the Executive (Adults Lane)

The antidote to the static nature of the boardroom and the torque of the golf swing. We utilize calculated mobility, breathwork (Wim Hof Method), and leverage-based defense to render you anti-fragile. This is sophisticated fitness for the high-performing mind.
