The "Kinetic Chess" Academy: Sensei Sandy BJJ

Location: Main Street, Tannersville (The Private Studio)

Discard the archaic notion of "fighting." True Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is physics applied to physiology. It is kinetic chess played with high stakes and higher rewards.

For the Legacy (Kids Lane)

Summer offers freedom, but freedom without structure breeds complacency. Our curriculum provides the necessary counter-weight to Onteora's ease. We introduce controlled adversity in a sterile, prestige environment - forging the strategic thinking and quiet confidence required to lead the next generation. Browse the beginner BJJ glossary.

For the Executive (Adults Lane)

The antidote to the static nature of the boardroom and the torque of the golf swing. We utilize calculated mobility, breathwork (Wim Hof Method), and leverage-based defense to render you anti-fragile. This is sophisticated fitness for the high-performing mind. Browse the beginner BJJ glossary.

The Velvet Rope

Optimization. Not Perspiration.

You optimize your portfolio. You optimize your operations. It is time to optimize your biology. The Adults Lane is a study in leverage and efficiency, not a brawl. We combine the cerebral complexity of grappling with the restorative power of breathwork. It is the perfect physical complement to a summer of golf and tennis - keeping your hips fluid, your back strong, and your mind sharp. Strictly Professional. Highly Technical. Zero Ego.
