Inside Tie

An inside tie is a standing hand-fighting connection where your arm or hand controls the inside line between your body and your partner's arm to improve positioning and limit their control.

Foundational concept Basics Common Inside Position Hand Fighting Collar Tie

Quick definition

An inside tie is a standing hand-fighting connection where your arm or hand controls the inside line between your body and your partner's arm to improve positioning and limit their control.

Beginner translation

Inside tie means winning the space on the inside so your arms are in a better lane than theirs.

Why it matters

An inside tie is a standing hand-fighting connection where your arm or hand controls the inside line between your body and your partner's arm to improve positioning and limit their control.

What beginners should know

A common mistake with inside tie is using it passively instead of with structure and timing.

Common class phrases

  • Inside tie connects to inside position
  • hand fighting
  • collar tie
  • pummel

Beginner Safety Cue

Safety cue: Move with control and communicate early while training inside tie.

FAQ

Is inside tie only for wrestlers?

No. Inside ties are useful in BJJ standing exchanges for control, posture, and setup safety.

Do inside ties work without footwork?

Not well. Feet, posture, and head position make the tie effective.
