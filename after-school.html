After School Can Become the Best Hour of Their Day

School ends. Energy is high. The evening still needs rhythm.

Jiu-Jitsu gives kids a simple pattern: arrive, breathe, move, listen, practice, partner safely, review, and leave proud.

The goal is calm confidence: better focus, better body control, and a stronger sense of belonging.

Focus

Students practice listening, following steps, and staying with a task.

Confidence

They build wins through safe reps, coaching, and steady progress.

Self-Control

They learn how to move with a partner using care and control.

Better Evenings

Movement before dinner helps the day reset with structure.

What Happens in Class

1. Arrival and Reset

Students come in, take off shoes, get settled, and shift out of school mode.

2. Warm-Up

Simple movement drills help loosen hips, backs, shoulders, and legs.

3. Skill of the Day

We teach one clear idea at a time so kids can remember it.

4. Partner Practice

Students work with a partner under close coaching. Safety and kindness come first.

5. Cool Down

We end with a simple review so students know what they learned.
