More than just "Coverage"

School lets out, you’re still working, and your child still has a ton of energy. Our After-School Jiu-Jitsu Training gives them a safe, supervised place to land instead of going straight to a couch and a screen.

At Sensei Sandy BJJ, kids:

  • Move their bodies after sitting all day
  • Practice respect, patience, and self-control
  • Learn how to fall safely and protect themselves
  • Finish class feeling tired in the right way, not fried

Consistent Routine

This is steady, week-after-week training. Most families start with our 6-Week Grand Slam to build habits, then slide into After-School for the long haul.

The Afternoon Rhythm

How a typical class flows from arrival to pickup.

Arrival & Reset

Check-in, shoes off, breathe, and settle from the school day.

Warm-up

Drills and games to loosen hips and backs after a day in a desk.

Technique

Clear, step-by-step fundamentals. Short instructions so they remember.

Supervised Rounds

Partner work with close coaching on safety, control, and kindness.

Cool Down

A "today I learned" share and clear focus for next time.

For parents, this means...

  • Reliable coverage:
    You know exactly where they are and what they’re doing.
  • Better evenings:
    They’ve burned energy, moved their bodies, and had real social time.
  • Stronger habits:
    Listening, being on time, taking care of gear, and respect.
  • Local community:
    Kids training together from Tannersville, Hunter, and nearby towns.

Who is this for?

  • Elementary & middle schoolers who need structure.
  • Families who want consistent training.
  • Graduates of our 6-Week Grand Slam.
  • Current students who want extra mat time.
