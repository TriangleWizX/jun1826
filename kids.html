6. Kids Beginner FAQ

Kids Beginner FAQ

Can my child start with a calm first class?
Yes. We pace the first class slowly and keep the room calm and structured.
Is this striking or grappling?
Grappling only. No punches or kicks in the kids lane. Kids learn body control, safe movement, and respectful partner work.
Can parents watch class?
Yes. Parents can watch and see exactly how class is run.
What class do most kids try first?
Most kids start in the 5:00 PM Youth Class. We group partners by size, comfort, and experience.
What should my child wear?
Comfortable athletic clothes are fine for the first visit. Sandy will help with gear after the Free Intro.
Where should I start if my child needs a quieter first step?
Read the first-timer guide, then text Sandy if you want help choosing between the youth class and a quieter private first step.

Your first step is on us.

Try a free Kids Intro. Tour the room, ask questions, and choose the next step.
