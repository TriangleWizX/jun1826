A Reset Button for Your Child

If you’re worried your child is glued to a tablet, shuts down in real situations, or melts down when things get hard, you’re not alone.

This is the "On-Ramp" to martial arts.

The Kids 6-Week Confidence Challenge is the dedicated youth version of our Grand Slam program. It is designed to get beginners ready.

(Note: Most families start with this 6-week program to build habits, then slide into our ongoing After-School program for long-term training.)

On our mats, kids don’t scroll. They bow in, listen, and try again. Through playful “porrada” (intense, focused practice), we teach them to stay present, follow instructions, and keep going even when something feels tricky.

What The Challenge Builds

Respect

Bowing in, saying “yes sir / yes ma’am,” taking turns, and taking care of training partners.

Discipline

Showing up twice a week, lining up on time, listening the first time, and finishing what they start.

Persistence

Trying again after a fall, problem-solving from tough positions, and learning that “hard” does not mean “I quit.”

Bully-Resistant Basics

Standing tall, using a strong voice, knowing when to walk away, and simple, safe escapes if someone grabs them.

Unshakable Confidence

The feeling of having “internal armor” instead of hiding behind a screen.

Safety & Standards

  • Clean, Supervised Mats: Clear rules, consistent routines, no chaos.
  • Age-Appropriate Drills: No headshots, no wild swings, always controlled contact.
  • Character First: Kindness, teamwork, and tapping early are built into every class.
  • Small Groups: Your child gets eye contact, coaching by name, and safe partners.
